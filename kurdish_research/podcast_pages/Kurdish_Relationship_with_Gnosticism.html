Description

The provided text offers a scholarly analysis exploring the potential historical and theological connections between Gnosticism and the Kurdish religious traditions of Yezidism and Yarsanism. It begins by outlining the core beliefs and historical context of Gnosticism, emphasizing its focus on esoteric knowledge and dualistic worldview.
