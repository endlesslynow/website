Description

Mount Lelun and the Afrin region of Syria are the central focus of these sources. The texts explore the area's geographical context, historical depth spanning millennia, and rich cultural tapestry, particularly its significance to Kurdish identity and diverse religious heritage. A key theme is the profound impact of the Syrian Civil War and the 2018 Turkish military operation ("Olive Branch"), which led to occupation, demographic shifts, human rights violations, and the endangerment of its cultural and environmental landscape. The sources collectively illustrate Mount Lelun's enduring strategic importance and its current status as a contested and transformed region.
