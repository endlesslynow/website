Afrin-Aleppo Relations

Download Audio File

Description

This report explores the complex relationship between the Afrin region and the city of Aleppo in northern Syria before 2010. It highlights Afrin's status as an administrative district within the Aleppo Governorate and examines the significant economic ties, particularly the critical olive trade supporting Aleppo's soap industry. Furthermore, the text describes the socio-demographic landscape of Afrin, characterized by a predominantly Kurdish population with cultural and kinship links to Aleppo, and notes the political undercurrents influenced by Kurdish nationalism despite state control. Overall, the sources illustrate a dynamic where Afrin was integrated into Aleppo's sphere while maintaining its own identity and structures prior to the Syrian conflict.
