El Tico - the Kurdish Story Teller from Afrin

Download Audio File

Description

These sources collectively provide a detailed overview of Mihemed Elî Tico, a highly influential Kurdish Dengbêj (traditional singer and storyteller) from the Afrin region of Syria. They highlight his significance as a keeper of Kurdish oral tradition and history through epic tales and songs, often performed with his tanbur. The texts discuss his life, including his local fame in Afrin and Aleppo, key artistic relationships, and his international recognition through UNESCO recordings and performances. Finally, they examine his diverse repertoire and discuss his enduring cultural legacy, preserved through recordings and digital platforms despite a lack of official state acknowledgment.
