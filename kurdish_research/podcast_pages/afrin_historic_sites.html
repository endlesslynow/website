Afrin's Historic Sites

Download Audio File

Description

The provided text meticulously documents the significant historical and archaeological heritage of the Afrin region in Syria, highlighting its importance as a crossroads of civilizations for millennia, evidenced by Paleolithic sites like Dederiyeh Cave and Iron Age structures such as Ain Dara Temple. The report details the devastating impact of the Syrian Civil War and the 2018 Turkish military intervention on this rich cultural landscape. It catalogs extensive damage to major sites like Ain Dara, Cyrrhus/Nabi Huri, and Barad, a UNESCO World Heritage site, resulting from military actions, widespread looting, and deliberate destruction. Furthermore, the text notes the vulnerability of numerous lesser-known tells and religious shrines, suggesting a systematic erosion of Afrin's diverse cultural identity, and concludes with urgent recommendations for monitoring, accountability, and future preservation efforts.
