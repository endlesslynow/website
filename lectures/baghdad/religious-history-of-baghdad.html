The Religious History of Baghdad

762 to 2026 · rulers, faiths, and sectarian conflict · lecture data checked against Marozzi and academic sources · hover anything for its source note
Base figures come from the Timeline of Baghdad and Peoples of Baghdad lecture pages, verified against Marozzi's Baghdad, Encyclopaedia Iranica, and other academic sources. Corrections applied where scholarship disagreed, see the verification log. Pre-modern shares are scholarly reconstructions at era resolution. Small Mandaean and other communities are noted with markers rather than bands. Christian share is first quantified from the 833 to 946 era. Works cited at the bottom of the page.
