Armenians of Afrin

Download Audio File

Description

The provided text meticulously chronicles the history and displacement of the Armenian community in Afrin, Syria, tracing their origins as survivors of the Armenian Genocide who sought refuge in the region. It details their peaceful coexistence within the Kurdish-majority area before the 2018 Turkish military offensive ("Operation Olive Branch"), which led to their forced removal and the occupation of Afrin. The sources further describe the demographic changes, human rights abuses, and cultural erasure under Turkish-backed control, concluding with the dismal prospects for the Armenians' return and their dispersal as part of a broader exodus from Syria.
