Exercise: Translate into Ottoman

Type the Ottoman translation for each English phrase below.

Note: You must use a Persian or Ottoman keyboard to be able to do this exercise.

  1. 1. Our; with me, on me; from us; your, with you, on you; to you; to him; with him, on him.
  2. 2. From him; himself etc.; from himself; his; to you.
  3. 3. Their milk [nom.], their milk [acc.]; in our coffee; from your house.
  4. 4. From his house; from him; (noun) his; (noun) of his house, (ev) his house [nom.].
  5. 5. My tea, your tea, their tea; our tea [acc.]; their tea [acc.]; his tea, the tea.
  6. 6. To us and to you, from us and from you, from them and from you.
  7. 7. There are large and beautiful trees in their, [in] our and [in] your garden.
  8. 8. I have no outdoor boots on my feet; you have boots on your feet.
  9. 9. Your foot, their feet, on their feet.
  10. 10. My water, in my water, your water, in their water.
  11. 11. Our water is very good, yours is bad (filthy) and scanty.
  12. 12. Both of us, three of us, and four of us are diligent, they are lazy.
  13. 13. Your children are in that house. Our children are in our house.
  14. 14. My shoes and boots; the shepherd's sandals and stick.
  15. 15. Your daughter's red slipper and black stockings.
  16. 16. His copy-book; in their copy-books. There is no coffee in their coffee-pot.
  17. 17. A cup of coffee; two cups of milk.
  18. 18. Your son; with (in) your son; with (in) our master; with (in) your master; in your eye.
