Hurrian and Hittite Afrin Remnants

Description

This report explores the archaeological evidence of Hurrian and Hittite civilizations in the Afrin region of Syria, a strategically important historical crossroads. It examines key sites like the Ain Dara temple and the Afrin Stele, highlighting their significance as Syro-Hittite monuments with demonstrable Hurrian cultural influence. The text also addresses the challenges of interpreting evidence in this area of significant cultural interaction and fusion, acknowledging claims about sites like Cyrrhus and Kolpe while emphasizing the need for substantiated archaeological data. Ultimately, it underscores the tragic impact of recent conflicts on the preservation of these ancient remains and the necessity for further research.
