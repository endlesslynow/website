Hopkins Method Language Learning

Seven Tips for the Hopkins Method:

  1. Do some sentences everyday - maximum 1 unit per day.
  2. It's OK to not know every word or understand every sentence. I promise.
  3. Use the "reverse" button to practice the language both ways.
  4. Do not try to memorize!
  5. Reviewing is OK. You don't need to always learn new material.
  6. The key is consistency and familiarity. Again, do some sentences every day.
  7. Be proud of yourself and have fun :)
